Malaria

    Info

    Malaria is a preodminant disease in developing countries. Developing Countries like Africa and the Philipines. Plasmodium is the infectious disease that causes malaria. Malaria is transmitted when a female mosquito carrying plasmodium bites or sucks the blood of a person. Mosquitos tend to breed in stagnant water.

Prevention

  • - wear long sleeves
  • - get vaccinations
  • - use mosquito nets
  • - use bug repellent
  • - stay away from stagnant water

    • Epidemiology

    History: first evidences of Malaria was found in Mosquitos that were perserved in amber from the palaeogene period (thats approximatly 30 million years old). Quinine was used to treat Malaria from the 1800's until around World War 2.
